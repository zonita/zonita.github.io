    2017/18 season is open for ideas

    Glenn Hutchings

    May 06, 2017

    Comments

    The Spring 2017 season is now over. We hope you'll agree there were some excellent movies!

    We are reviewing our Autumn 2017 and Spring 2018 movie line up, both matinee and evening. All ideas and suggestions are welcome, whether a classic, thriller, hollywood, or musical. Why not write and let us know? You can either leave a comment on this page, or email us at info@zonita.org.uk.
