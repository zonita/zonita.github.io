    Last showing is cancelled due to Coronavirus

    Glenn Hutchings

    Mar 18, 2020

    Comments

    You won't be too surprised to hear that we've decided to cancel the April showings at the Zonita, due to the ongoing Coronavirus outbreak. Apologies to all who were looking forward to those movies; we may move them to the start of the next season instead (planned for September). Until then, stay safe!
