    Tickets

    Go here to read about how we will be keeping you as safe as possible from Covid 19.

    On film days, there are usually two showings: Matinee (2pm) and Evening (7pm). Doors open 45 minutes before the film starts. Ticket prices are:

    • £3 for children
    • £3 for adults (matinee)
    • £5 for adults (evening)

    You can buy tickets on the door, or reserve tickets and tables (at no extra charge) by emailing us at zonitacinema@gmail.com with the following details:

    • Which event (date and showing) you want to reserve for.
    • If you want to reserve tickets, how many adults and children.
    • If you want to reserve a table, how many people it's for.
    • Your name, so we can put it on your reservation.

    Tickets and tables are reserved on a first-come-first-served basis. Book early to avoid disappointment!
